Founder & Visionary

Olisa Agbakoba

Senior Advocate of Nigeria (SAN)

A pioneer of Nigerian human rights law and a titan of commercial arbitration, Dr. Olisa Agbakoba SAN established OAL with a mandate to bridge the gap between rigorous legal tradition and the dynamic needs of a globalised economy.

His legacy spans maritime innovation, space law advocacy, and constitutional reform — shaping the very fabric of the Nigerian legal landscape for over four decades. A former President of the Nigerian Bar Association, his influence extends well beyond the courtroom.

Dr. Agbakoba's vision was simple but profound: a law firm that does not just advise — but architects outcomes that endure far beyond its own lifetime.

Managing Partner

Yvonne Ezekiel

Managing Partner · OAL

"Drawing upon our extensive background in law and our profound comprehension of business dynamics, we have endeavoured to establish OAL as a firm that surpasses expectations in client service — making a meaningful impact that endures far beyond our lifetimes."

As Managing Partner, Yvonne Ezekiel directs the firm's strategic growth and operational excellence. With a focus on corporate governance and complex litigation, she ensures OAL remains at the vanguard of legal service delivery in Nigeria and across Africa.

Her leadership is characterised by a people-first philosophy — fostering an environment where multidisciplinary expertise meets unwavering ethical standards and client-centricity.
