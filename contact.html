Common Questions

Frequently Asked

Can't find what you're looking for? Our team is always ready to assist.

Email Us Directly
How do I book an initial consultation? +
Fill out the contact form on this page or call our headquarters directly. Our practice administrator will review your inquiry and pair you with the most relevant partner within 24 hours. You can also request a virtual meeting if you prefer.
What are your consultation fees? +
Fees vary depending on the complexity of the matter and the seniority of the attorney involved. A standard engagement fee is discussed during the preliminary call. We are transparent about all fees upfront — there are no hidden charges.
Do you offer virtual consultations? +
Yes. We conduct high-level briefings via secure video conferencing platforms for international clients and those unable to visit our physical offices. Simply select "Virtual Meeting" as your preferred contact method in the form above.
What documents should I bring to my consultation? +
To maximise the value of our time together, please provide all relevant contracts, correspondence, or court documents via our secure client portal before the meeting. Our team will review them in advance so we can hit the ground running.
How quickly will I receive a response? +
All inquiries submitted via the form or by email are acknowledged within 24 hours on business days. For urgent matters, we strongly recommend calling our 24/7 support line: +234 803 724 2453.
Is my information kept confidential? +
Absolutely. Attorney-client confidentiality is fundamental to our practice. All information shared with OAL — whether via form, email, or phone — is treated with the strictest professional discretion in accordance with Nigerian Bar Association rules.
