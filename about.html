About OAL

Nigeria's Leading
World-Class Legal Partner

Olisa Agbakoba Legal (OAL) is a tier-one law firm in Lagos, Nigeria and a leading, world-class legal solutions provider with clients in diverse sectors of the Nigerian economy.

Providing innovative legal solutions is basic to who we are and what we stand for. Our diversified skills ensure that our clients' commercial needs are satisfied promptly and professionally, always conforming to high ethical standards.

Structured as a partnership, the firm operates through diverse practice groups, each headed by a partner — ensuring that members of each group are well-suited and trained to proffer ingenious contributions. We believe in teamwork to achieve work that satisfies both the firm and the client.

Our Story

Our Historical Journey

OAL is a leading law firm in Nigeria that has grown from a sole proprietorship to a bustling practice offering innovative legal solutions to all sectors in Nigeria's emerging economy.

The focus of OAL has always been to push through new boundaries to find solutions to legal issues and win the confidence of its clients. At OAL, we are committed to excellence and this has kept us in pace with global trends.

Founded

Established as a Sole Proprietorship

Dr. Olisa Agbakoba SAN founded the firm with a vision to bridge rigorous legal tradition with the dynamic needs of Nigeria's emerging economy.

Growth

Expansion into a Full Partnership

The firm grew into a structured partnership with specialized practice groups, each led by an experienced partner to ensure depth and excellence.

Recognition

Ranked as a Tier-One Law Firm

OAL earned its Tier One ranking through consistent delivery of world-class legal solutions, earning 50+ awards and recognition across the continent.

Today

Africa's Premier Legal Solutions Provider

With offices in Lagos, Abuja, and Port Harcourt, OAL continues to set the benchmark for legal innovation, client service, and continental impact.
